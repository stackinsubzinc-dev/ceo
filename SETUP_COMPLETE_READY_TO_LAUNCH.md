## 🔧 CONNECTING A NEW PLATFORM (Example: Shopify)

1. Go to Vault → Find "Shopify" card → Click "Connect"

2. Open Shopify admin:
   - Settings → Apps and Integrations → Develop Apps
   - Create new app
   - Name: `FiiLTHY Sync`
   - Permissions needed:
     - ✓ read_products
     - ✓ read_orders
     - ✓ read_inventory

3. Copy your **Admin Access Token**

4. Back in FiiLTHY:
   - Paste token
   - Paste store URL (e.g., `mystore.myshopify.com`)
   - Click "Test Connection"
   - ✓ Success!

5. Go to **Products** page → Your Shopify products appear!
